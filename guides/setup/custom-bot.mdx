## Creating a Bot

<Steps>
<Step title="Creating an Bot">
	> First, headover to [Discord Developer Page](https://discord.com/developers/applications) and create a new Application.
	<Frame>
		<img src="/images/setup_create_application.png" />
	</Frame>
	<Note>Give your Application an appropriate name, the name will be displayed to your members.</Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Creating a bot">
	<Frame>
		<img src="/images/setup_create_bot.png" />
	</Frame>
	<Tip>You may name the bot to anything you want, just make sure you use an appropriate name. This name will be displayed on the member list.</Tip>
</Step>

</Steps>

## Adding your bot to RestoreCord

<Card title="Shortcut to create a Custom Bot" icon="link" color="#2ecc70" href="https://restorecord.com/dashboard/custombots/new" horizontal="true">
	Use this button to create a Custom Bot faster!
</Card>
> Headover to the Dashboard, select **Custom Bots** in the navigtion bar and **click create** then follow the requested information shown on the dashboard.

<Frame>
	<img src="/images/dashboard_customBots_create.png" />
</Frame>

## Setup Redirect URL

> After adding your bot on the dashboard, you need to add our Redirect Url on Discords Developer Page as shown:

<Frame caption="Application -> OAuth2 -> Add Redirect">
	<img src="/images/setup_redirect-url.png" />
</Frame>
```text Redirect URI
https://restorecord.com/api/callback
```
