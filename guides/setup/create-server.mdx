		Use this shortcut to create a server faster!
	</Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Custom Bot">
    <Note>
        You have to close the popup window in order to continue.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your Discord Server">
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fix Permissions">
    > After inviting the bot, make sure that the integrated bot role is above the verify:
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/setup_bot-permissions.png" />
    </Frame>
    >
    <Warning>
        You cant give the bot admin role, **you must put the integrated bot role above the verify role.**
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the verify role">
    > Select the role you want your members to receive after verifying with RestoreCord.
    <Note>
        If your roles dont show up then make sure that the integrated bot role is above the verified role. Click the **click here** button on the bottom to refresh the roles list.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check> You have succesfully added your Discord Server to RestoreCord. </Check>
