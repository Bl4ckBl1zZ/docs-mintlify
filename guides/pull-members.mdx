---
title: "How to Pull your Members"
description: "A step-by-step guide about pulling your members into different Discord Servers."
icon: "users-medical"
---

> Go to the [Servers](https://restr.co/servers) page and click Migrate on the server you want to pull members from.
<Frame>
    <img src="images/setup_pull_migrate_button.png" alt="Migrate Button" />
</Frame>

> 
<Info>
    Make sure you have invited the Bot to the server you want to pull members to.
</Info>

> Once the popup appears, You can choose which **Discord** server you want to pull the members to.
<Frame>
    <img src="images/setup_pull_server.png" alt="Migrate Server Select" />
</Frame>

> After you select the server, You can optionally choose if you want to pull the members with a specific role.
<Frame>
    <img src="images/setup_pull_role.png" alt="Migrate Role Select" />
</Frame>

> You can also specify how many members you want to pull from the server, the Bot will automatically stop once it reaches the amount you specified.
<Frame>
    <img src="images/setup_pull_amount.png" alt="Migrate Amount Select" />
</Frame>

>
<Info>
    If the Bot doesn't pull the specific amount you specified, It means that the server couldn't find more members to pull.
</Info>

> Once you're done, Click **Pull** and the Bot will start pulling the members.
<Frame>
    <img src="images/setup_pull_success.png" alt="Migrate Success" />
</Frame>
